Who Are we Trying to Reach?

Who: investors, businesses, advertisers, and local citizens
Age: 16+
Technology: We'll want the page to be user friendly and easy to access from either casual mobile browsing, or more desktop oriented views
Motivation: To educate potential investors and citizens about the town and it's history, relevance, and potential future.

Personas

Persona #1 Vlad Chlouse


Vlad is a local business man who owns a moderate Pest Control company that has operated in the East Valley for 20+ years. He has a Master's of Business from ASU and employs at least 50 pest technicians locally. Vlad is interested in advertising and continuing his relationships with other local business leaders.
Occupation: CEO of Pest Busters
Demographics: 43, owner of local business
Goals and motivation: Create relationships with other local businesses, as well as advertising his own services.
Social: Family man just had 2nd child. Very active, fitness buff, hunter, hiking, camping, boating.
Technology: Technology literate, typically uses a phone, but sometimes uses desktop browser both Apple based.

Persona #2 Josephine Silver


Josephine is an up-and-coming real estate investor who recently obtained her real estate license. She has lived around Apache Junction for years and knows the geography and local housing market well.
Occupation: Owner of Mountain Real Estate
Demographics: 32, real estate investor
Goals and motivation: Create relationships with other local businesses, advertising her own services, and getting to know the local city better.
Social: Recently married, active, hiking, camping, gaming, shooting.
Technology: Technology literate, typically uses a phone, but sometimes uses desktop browser both Apple based.

Scenarios

1. What kind of city is Apache Junction? How large is it? What services are available?
2. Who are currently members of the local Chamber of Commerce? Why would I want to be one? How can I?
3. What can the Chamber of Commerce do for me? What can I do to help the city of Apache Junction?
4. Did the city hire a good web development team to make a well made site?
5. How do I get in touch with the Chamber of Commerce?
