-Site Purpose-


The main purpose of superstitioncity.com will be to serve as a chamber of commerce site for the ficitional city of Superstition located in Arizona near it's namesake of the Superstition Mountains. Chambers of commerce are typically groups of local businesses that band together to create relaitonships between themselves and the town, other businesses, and possible future benificiaries.

The information will be provided thoughout several pages with an emphasis on a clean, good user experience with repsonsive elements, and other UX based design features. Some of the content that will be presented includes: a home page, a discover page, a directory, a join page, and a contact page. Each of these will serve different purposes for the site ranging in use from current demographics and local history, to a page dedicated to handling memberships to the chamber of commerce.
